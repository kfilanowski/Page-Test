History

All Knowledge Skills can be used to glean relevant History for their subject matters.
The includes recalling major events, figures, artifacts, and deciphering hieroglyphs.

Difficulty DC Example
Mundane 0 Recalling an event/creature that happened/existed in the past 20 years.
Requires Focus 10 Recalling an event/creature that happened/existed in the past 20-99 years.
Trained 20 Identifying how a ritual took place in an ancient ruin, and how you might activate these mechanisms

Understanding the roundabout significance of murals and other arts
Difficult 30 Recalling an event/creature that happened 100+ years ago
Very Difficult 40 Understanding the roundabout significance of heiroglyphs/ancient language
Masterwork 50 Creating full translations of hieroglyphs.
