Identify Spell

By observing a Spell as it is being cast, you can Instantly use Arcana to identify which Spell it is. The DC is 5 per level of the Technique (not including Upcast.)

This is useful for determining whether you want to take action in response, or it has no immediate effect - though many Spells do not need to be identified if you already know the spell.

You may use Astronomy to identify Lunar spells, and Nature to identify Gaia spells.
