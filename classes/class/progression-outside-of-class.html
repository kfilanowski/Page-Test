Progression Outside of Class

Boons/Banes - Gained at level one as a result of having positive or negative luck.

Feats - Gained at level 1, and every odd level after.

Technique Points - Gain one that can be used on any school of techniques at level 2, and every even level after.

Achievement - Can be gained at any time, even retroactively, when certain conditions are met, varying based on the achievement.
