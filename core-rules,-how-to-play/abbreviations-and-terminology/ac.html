AC

Avoidability Capacity (AC) is the measure of how difficult it is to hit you. Targets must meet or exceed your AC with an Attack to Damage you or succeed at a Combat Maneuver.

By default, your starting AC is 10, and you add your LV, DEX, LUK, and Armor, and adjust it based on Circumstance.
