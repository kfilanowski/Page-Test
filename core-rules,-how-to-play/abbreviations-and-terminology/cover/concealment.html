Concealment

Concealment is a Circumstance that is gained when a creature's position is being obscured from another's Senses.

Usually, this means an effect like fog or darkness is partially or totally hiding a creature from another, resulting in Partial Concealment or Total Concealment, but this may take other forms against creatures that have other senses.

Concealment is a condition that's relative to the senses of other creatures. If you have Dark Vision and your opponent doesn't in a situation with Darkness, you have concealment against them, but they don't have concealment to you.

Attacking from concealment, even Total Concealment, does not Surprise by default. You still need to use Stealth as normal.
