Damage

Damage is a value that subtracts from a target's HP when the effect takes place.
"Losing HP" is synonymous with taking Damage.

Damage is always assigned a type. The physical damage types are Crush, Slash, and Pierce, and the elemental types are Magic, Lightning, Fire, Cold, Holy, Poison, and Psychic. These are also Tags.

For Weapons, this value is determined by the weapon used and adds your STR. A Technique may add to this as well.
For Spells, this value is determined by your LV and Spell Power.
Any given effect will describe how to determine the damage it deals.
